    WELCOME TO NIELIT IMPHAL OFFICIAL WEBSITE

NIELIT Imphal, formerly known as CEDT/CEDTI/DOEACC, officially began operations in July 1989 with the launch of its Three-Year Diploma in Electronics Engineering program. In addition to the main center, NIELIT Imphal has two extension centers located in Churachandpur and Senapati districts of Manipur. Across India, there are a total of 43 NIELIT Centers, with 21 located in the North-Eastern region alone.

The NIELIT Imphal campus is situated in Akampat, around 5 km southeast of Imphal city center. It spans over 20 acres and includes a variety of facilities such as administrative offices, lecture halls, faculty rooms, computer labs, a mechanical workshop, and several specialized labs focusing on Information, Electronics, and Communication Technology. In addition to the main building, the campus also features staff quarters, student hostels, an electrical substation, and other essential amenities.

As part of its mission to contribute to national development, NIELIT Imphal offers both long-term and short-term skill development courses in Electronics & Communication Engineering and Information Technology. The center’s goal is to improve the employability of youth, both within the state and across the country, in line with the Government of India’s National Skill Development Policy.

The long-term courses offered at NIELIT Imphal include the AICTE-recognized Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering, Diploma in Computer Science & Engineering, Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications (PGDCA), Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), Master of Computer Applications (MCA), and Master of Science in Information Technology (MSc.IT), all of which are affiliated with Manipur University (a Central University). Additionally, the center offers 'O' Level courses in IT, Hardware, and Multimedia in the non-formal sector, which lead to government-recognized certificates or post-graduate diplomas.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, NIELIT Imphal swiftly transitioned to online classes for all its courses. To ensure high-quality delivery, the center uses MOODLE as its Learning Management System (LMS), along with various other tools to support both synchronous and asynchronous learning.

Since its establishment in 1988, NIELIT Imphal has trained over 4,000 students in formal university-affiliated courses, more than 6,500 in non-formal NIELIT courses, and over 50,000 candidates through various short-term programs.

The present activities of the centre include:

  • Long Term (Formal and non-Formal)
  • Short Term Courses in ICT & Electronics
  • Consultancy Services in IECT
  • Corporate Training Programme for State / Central Govt. Deptts
  • Training partner of Skill Council for Green Jobs & Electronics Sector Skill Councils.

NIELIT Imphal is particularly known for its skill development programs in Electronics, IT, and Renewable Energy, with most courses aligned with the National Skills Qualification Framework (NSQF). ACCREDITATION

Under the Accreditation Scheme of NIELIT, computer – training institutes in the non-formal sector, meeting well-defined criteria, are granted accreditation for specific levels of NIELIT courses under viz. O (Foundation) Level, A (Advanced Diploma) Level, B Level (Equivalent to MCA) and C Level (Equivalent to M.Tech). The institutes desirous of getting Provisional Accreditation for any level of course under the NIELIT Scheme are required to apply online on the URL accreditation.nielit.gov.in
